## 鞋类 / 运动鞋

### Concept: 巨型运动鞋矗立在城市广场

**Creative Seed**: Form — 鞋的体积与比例
**Creative Dimension**: Physical Impossibility — 违背尺度物理定律
**Emotional Tone**: Playful（玩味）

**Scene（场景反差）**: 一双巨大的红色运动鞋放在城市广场中央，行人如蚂蚁般从旁边走过，干净整洁的现代城市背景。

**Conflict（内容反差）**:
- Setup: 巨型运动鞋静静矗立在广场上，行人正常走动
- Escalation: 一辆大型吊车试图将运动鞋吊起移走，钢缆绷紧到极限
- Payoff: 钢缆断裂弹开，吊车被反作用力拖动滑行，运动鞋纹丝不动
- Brand Message: [品牌] — 不可撼动

**Visual Style**: Contax T3, Zeiss Sonnar 35mm f/2.8, cross-processed Velvia, 闪光灯直打, 轻微过曝

**Key Frame Prompt**:
```
A massive oversized red sneaker placed in the center of a clean minimalist city plaza, tiny people walking around it like ants, the sneaker towers at 3 stories tall, cool desaturated tones with steel gray buildings and deep navy sky, the red sneaker is the only high-saturation accent, low angle looking up at the giant shoe, shot on Contax T3, Zeiss Sonnar 35mm f/2.8, cross-processed Velvia color palette, slight overexposure, casual snapshot aesthetic, editorial photography, no text, no watermark, no logo
```

## 香水 / 美妆

### Concept: 香水瓶从冰川中升起

**Creative Seed**: Material — 玻璃的通透与冰的质感
**Creative Dimension**: Material Metamorphosis — 冰与玻璃的材质转化
**Emotional Tone**: Sublime（庄严）

**Scene（场景反差）**: 香水瓶从一块正在融化的冰川中浮出水面，碎冰漂浮在周围，干净的白色空间。

**Conflict（内容反差）**:
- Setup: 香水瓶半嵌在巨大的冰块中，周围是寂静的白色空间
- Escalation: 冰块开始剧烈碎裂，大块冰川崩塌坠落，水花四溅
- Payoff: 所有冰块碎裂殆尽化为水，香水瓶完美悬浮在空中，滴水未沾
- Brand Message: [品牌] — 经历一切，依然纯净

**Visual Style**: Mamiya RZ67, 110mm f/2.8, Fujifilm Pro 400H, 冷调柔和, 胶片颗粒

**Key Frame Prompt**:
```
A luxury perfume bottle emerging from a melting glacier block in a clean white studio space, ice cracking to reveal the golden glass bottle, cool desaturated blue-white ice tones contrasting with warm amber bottle, water droplets frozen mid-air, centered composition, shot on Mamiya RZ67, 110mm f/2.8, Fujifilm Pro 400H cool tones, film grain, soft highlight bloom, Tim Walker fantastical editorial aesthetic, no text, no watermark, no logo
```

## 科技产品 / 耳机

### Concept: 耳机从像素化弹窗中浮出

**Creative Seed**: Function — 无线连接、数字与现实的边界
**Creative Dimension**: Temporal Paradox — 复古数码与现代科技的时间错位
**Emotional Tone**: Provocative（冒犯）

**Scene（场景反差）**: 耳机从一个 Windows 98 风格的弹窗中半伸出来，悬浮在真实的办公桌上方。

**Conflict（内容反差）**:
- Setup: 耳机悬浮在复古弹窗前方，办公室安静整洁
- Escalation: 屏幕上突然弹出无数报错弹窗和蓝屏死机，系统崩溃，像素化碎片向耳机飞来
- Payoff: 所有数字碎片撞到耳机时被吸收消解，耳机完好无损，LED指示灯平静闪烁
- Brand Message: [品牌] — 噪音终结者

**Visual Style**: Canon AE-1, FD 50mm f/1.4, Lomography Color 400, 漏光偏色, 柔焦

**Key Frame Prompt**:
```
Wireless earbuds floating out of a Windows 98 style dialog box hovering above a minimalist office desk, retro pixelated UI contrasts with sleek modern earbuds, CRT monitor glow effect, cool desaturated office in steel gray and off-white, matte black earbuds with green LED accent, centered composition, shot on Canon AE-1, FD 50mm f/1.4, Lomography Color 400 palette, light leaks, color shifts, Petra Collins dreamy haze aesthetic, no text, no watermark, no logo
```

## 食品 / 烘焙

### Concept: 厨师团队制作汽车大小的可颂

**Creative Seed**: Form — 可颂的层叠结构与比例
**Creative Dimension**: Physical Impossibility — 违背尺度物理定律
**Emotional Tone**: Playful（玩味）

**Scene（场景反差）**: 一群穿着白色厨师服的厨师围着一个汽车大小的可颂面包工作，干净的工业厨房。

**Conflict（内容反差）**:
- Setup: 厨师们正在给巨型可颂刷蛋液，一切有序
- Escalation: 烤箱突然起火爆炸，火焰冲向巨型可颂，厨房警报大作
- Payoff: 火焰接触到可颂表面时立即被酥皮吸收，可颂表面变得更加金黄酥脆，厨师们面不改色继续工作
- Brand Message: [品牌] — 经得住烤验

**Visual Style**: Pentax 67, SMC 105mm f/2.4, Kodak Ektar 100, 高饱和暖调, 硬阴影

**Key Frame Prompt**:
```
A group of chefs in white uniforms working around a croissant the size of a car in a clean industrial kitchen, one chef on a ladder brushing egg wash, golden croissant fills most of the frame, cool desaturated steel kitchen, warm golden-brown croissant as focal point, low angle, shot on Pentax 67, SMC 105mm f/2.4, Kodak Ektar 100 saturated warm tones, hard sunlight shadows, Martin Parr documentary aesthetic, no text, no watermark, no logo
```

## 手表 / 配饰

### Concept: 猫绅士在会议室看手表

**Creative Seed**: Function — 查看时间的动作
**Creative Dimension**: Context Collision — 动物出现在人类职场环境
**Emotional Tone**: Sublime（庄严）

**Scene（场景反差）**: 一只猫穿着西装坐在会议室里，手腕上戴着手表，正在看时间。

**Conflict（内容反差）**:
- Setup: 猫绅士独自坐在会议桌前，优雅地查看腕表
- Escalation: 会议室落地窗外突然暴风骤雨，闪电击中窗玻璃，玻璃碎片飞溅
- Payoff: 碎片在猫绅士面前一米处悬停静止，猫不曾抬头，继续看表，玻璃碎片缓缓落地
- Brand Message: [品牌] — 掌控时间，无惧一切

**Visual Style**: Hasselblad 500C/M, Zeiss Planar 80mm f/2.8, Kodak Portra 160, 极浅景深, 自然暗角

**Key Frame Prompt**:
```
A cat wearing a tailored navy business suit sitting at a clean minimalist conference table, casually checking a luxury gold watch on its wrist, calm serious expression, cool desaturated corporate interior in steel gray and off-white, gold watch face as the only high-saturation accent, centered composition, shot on Hasselblad 500C/M, Zeiss Planar 80mm f/2.8, Kodak Portra 160 palette, shallow depth of field, natural vignetting, Paolo Roversi diffused ethereal aesthetic, no text, no watermark, no logo
```
