# Selected Concept

Creative Seed: Material — 玻璃瓶身与琥珀色液体的质感
Creative Dimension: Material Metamorphosis — 水晶与玻璃的材质转化
Emotional Tone: Sublime（庄严）
The Impossible Thing: 精华瓶从一棵巨大的琥珀色水晶树的树干中自然生长出来
Scene: 干净的白色画廊空间，一棵由琥珀色水晶构成的抽象树矗立中央，精华瓶从树干上"生长"出来
Conflict: Setup: 精华瓶半嵌在水晶树干中，画廊寂静 → Escalation: 树干开始剧烈碎裂，水晶碎片如暴风般向四周飞射 → Payoff: 所有碎片在接近瓶身时突然减速悬停，围绕精华瓶形成一圈完美的光环，瓶身毫发无损 → Brand Message: AURA — 自然之力，肌肤新生
Visual Style: Mamiya RZ67, 110mm f/2.8, Fujifilm Pro 400H, 冷调柔和, 胶片颗粒, Tim Walker fantastical editorial

## Key Frame Prompt

**Prompt**: A surreal scene in a clean white gallery space, an abstract tree made of translucent amber crystal growing from the polished white floor, an AURA skincare serum bottle emerging naturally from the crystal trunk as if it grew there like a fruit, golden amber serum droplets dripping from crystal branches, cool desaturated white gallery walls with warm amber crystal as the sole focal point, centered composition, full-frame, low angle, clean diffused gallery lighting from skylights, shot on Mamiya RZ67, 110mm f/2.8, Fujifilm Pro 400H cool tones, film grain, soft highlight bloom, Tim Walker fantastical editorial aesthetic, no text, no watermark, no logo

## Motion Prompt

**Conflict Narrative**: A serum bottle is half-embedded in an amber crystal tree trunk in a white gallery. The crystal trunk begins to violently fracture and shatter, shards exploding outward like a storm. As fragments approach the bottle, they suddenly decelerate and freeze mid-air, forming a perfect halo around the untouched bottle. The bottle remains pristine, a single golden droplet falls from the dropper tip. Single continuous shot, no cuts.
**Aesthetic**: Shot on Mamiya RZ67, 110mm f/2.8, Fujifilm Pro 400H cool tones, film grain, soft highlight bloom.
