# 剧集类型 → 音乐风格映射

根据影视剧类型推荐适配的音乐风格、乐器和人声方向。

## 映射表

### 古装言情

- **OP 风格**: epic Chinese pop, traditional-modern fusion, driving beat, guzheng + electronic drums
- **ED 风格**: intimate ballad, acoustic, sparse, piano/guzheng lead
- **角色曲**: Chinese pop ballad, orchestral strings, traditional instruments accent
- **BGM 调色板**: guzheng, erhu, dizi, pipa + orchestral strings, piano
- **人声倾向**: female breathy/clear, male deep/restrained
- **情感基调**: 虐恋、宿命、深情

### 都市甜宠

- **OP 风格**: bright pop, upbeat, synth pop, catchy hook, feel-good
- **ED 风格**: warm acoustic, indie pop, gentle, sweet
- **角色曲**: light pop, R&B, playful, modern production
- **BGM 调色板**: ukulele, acoustic guitar, light percussion, glockenspiel, synth pad
- **人声倾向**: female sweet/bright, male warm/gentle
- **情感基调**: 甜蜜、轻松、心动

### 霸总/豪门

- **OP 风格**: cinematic pop, powerful, orchestral, modern, dramatic
- **ED 风格**: emotional ballad, piano-driven, soaring vocals
- **角色曲**: cinematic pop, power ballad, string-heavy, grand
- **BGM 调色板**: grand piano, full orchestra, electronic beats, dramatic percussion
- **人声倾向**: female powerful/soaring, male authoritative/deep
- **情感基调**: 权力、欲望、隐忍、深情

### 悬疑/推理

- **OP 风格**: dark electronic, ambient, tension-building, minor key, glitch
- **ED 风格**: atmospheric, sparse, haunting, ethereal vocal
- **角色曲**: dark cinematic, psychological, minimal, unsettling
- **BGM 调色板**: low synth drone, sparse piano (minor key), tension strings, prepared piano, reverse effects
- **人声倾向**: whispered, distorted, ethereal, androgynous
- **情感基调**: 不安、疑惑、恐惧、揭秘

### 复仇/逆袭

- **OP 风格**: intense rock/electronic, aggressive, driving, empowering
- **ED 风格**: dark ballad, defiant, restrained anger turning to resolve
- **角色曲**: power ballad (主角), dark orchestral (反派), emotional pop (配角)
- **BGM 调色板**: electric guitar, heavy drums, orchestral brass, synth bass, war drums
- **人声倾向**: female defiant/powerful, male intense/raspy
- **情感基调**: 愤怒、决心、隐忍、爆发

### 仙侠/玄幻

- **OP 风格**: epic orchestral + Chinese traditional, soaring, mythical, grand scale
- **ED 风格**: ethereal, dreamy, guqin/xiao lead, meditative yet emotional
- **角色曲**: guofeng pop, mythical atmosphere, wide dynamic range
- **BGM 调色板**: guqin, xiao, pipa, orchestral strings, choir, ethereal synth pads, wind chimes
- **人声倾向**: female ethereal/clear, male powerful/operatic, wide range
- **情感基调**: 超凡脱俗、宏大、悲壮、仙气

### 年代/谍战

- **OP 风格**: cinematic, retro-modern fusion, jazz/big band elements, suspense
- **ED 风格**: vintage ballad, warm, nostalgic, analog feel
- **角色曲**: period-appropriate style, jazz, swing, blues influence
- **BGM 调色板**: jazz trio (piano/bass/drums), muted trumpet, vintage strings, typewriter percussion, radio static
- **人声倾向**: male rich/baritone, female warm/smoky
- **情感基调**: 怀旧、暗流涌动、忠诚、牺牲

### 家庭/伦理

- **OP 风格**: warm pop, heartfelt, accessible, sing-along quality
- **ED 风格**: gentle folk/acoustic, intimate, reflective
- **角色曲**: soft pop, acoustic, emotional sincerity
- **BGM 调色板**: acoustic guitar, piano, soft strings, harmonica, music box
- **人声倾向**: warm, natural, conversational, emotionally honest
- **情感基调**: 温情、矛盾、成长、和解

### 喜剧/搞笑

- **OP 风格**: funky, playful, catchy, bouncy, brass accents
- **ED 风格**: light indie, quirky, ukulele/whistling
- **角色曲**: comedic variations matching character quirks
- **BGM 调色板**: pizzicato strings, xylophone, tuba, kazoo, hand claps, finger snaps, slide whistle
- **人声倾向**: playful, exaggerated, fun
- **情感基调**: 滑稽、欢乐、荒诞、温暖

### 军旅/主旋律

- **OP 风格**: march tempo, brass-heavy, patriotic, powerful, anthem-like
- **ED 风格**: emotional ballad, orchestral, noble, sacrificial
- **角色曲**: military march (群像), emotional ballad (个人)
- **BGM 调色板**: military snare, brass ensemble, full orchestra, choir, bugle
- **人声倾向**: male powerful/bass-baritone, choir support
- **情感基调**: 壮烈、荣誉、牺牲、家国

## 情感节拍 → BGM 映射

通用映射，不依赖剧集类型：

| 情感节拍 | 风格标签 | 核心乐器 | 节奏 | 动态 |
|----------|----------|----------|------|------|
| 紧张 | tense, suspenseful, building | tremolo strings, low brass, timpani | 中速渐快 | 渐强 |
| 浪漫 | warm, intimate, romantic | piano, acoustic guitar, soft strings | 慢速 | 平稳柔和 |
| 喜剧 | playful, bouncy, quirky | pizzicato, xylophone, light percussion | 中快 | 跳跃 |
| 揭秘 | dramatic, revelation, shocking | full orchestra, choir accent, piano hit | 突变 | 静→爆 |
| 打斗 | intense, aggressive, driving | percussion-heavy, brass, electric guitar | 快速 | 持续高能 |
| 背叛 | dark, painful, hollow | solo cello, minor piano, sparse | 慢速 | 稀疏冰冷 |
| 重逢 | uplifting, emotional, hopeful | strings ensemble, piano, gentle choir | 中速渐快 | 渐强暖化 |
| 牺牲 | epic, noble, sorrowful | full orchestra, solo violin, choir | 中速 | 宏大悲壮 |
| 回忆 | nostalgic, dreamy, bittersweet | music box, piano, soft strings | 慢速 | 轻柔飘渺 |
| 决心 | resolute, empowering, building | drums, brass, driving strings | 中速→快 | 层层递进 |
